About Sundara HeART

Hi, I’m Sundara Heart — a high-desert mystic, painter, clay sorcerer, sonic alchemist, and INELDA-trained death doula, based in Santa Fe, New Mexico.

I create because the universe is pure chaos until I wrestle it into something that breathes. I chase raw emotion through thick oils that drip like desert sunsets, encaustic wax that burns and glows, acrylic explosions, digital glitches that feel alive, and hand-thrown clay pieces you can actually grip — vessels that hold stories, rituals, and late-night revelations.

Then I throw it all into sound. My music is experimental electronica and glitch-soaked soundscapes — think XXII with its orbit-melting tracks like “Gobbledy Orbit Meltdown,” “Cognitive Dissonance Hues,” and “Where Is My Mind,” plus fresh singles like Be Back Soon, Respected Sky, and Itty Bitty Sunshine that hit like cosmic static straight from the ether. (Stream it on Spotify and feel the frequency shift.)

But I don’t stop at the studio. Through Journeying On, I work as a death doula — guiding souls and families across life’s fiercest threshold with the same creative fire, turning endings into sacred, beautiful art.

And because the work wants to live everywhere, I’ve spun it all into merch — apparel, prints, talismans, and everyday objects drenched in the exact colors, symbols, and wild energy of my paintings, sculptures, and songs.

So whether you need a painting that sets your wall on fire, a clay piece that becomes your daily altar, a track that rewires your brain at 2 a.m., doula support for the big transition, or merch that lets you carry the magic with you…

Step in. Let it hit different. I hope my work leaves stardust on your soul.
